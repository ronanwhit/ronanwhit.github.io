ABOUT

Emi See is a California-born pianist, musician, improviser and collaborator based in Glasgow.
Equally versed in classical as well as contemporary idioms, she has performed through the U.S., U.K. and in Italy with performances of new works, world premieres, and classical repertoire. She has had the opportunity to work with and study with composers and artists such as Hie-Yon Choi, Shiori Usui, Peter Takacs, and others, as well as artists and creatives in Glasgow.
Emi is currently pursuing her undergraduate degree at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland with Aaron Shorr and Sinae Lee.
